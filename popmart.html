What is POP MART & POP NOW?

POP MART is a global lifestyle company that sells collectible figurines through "blind boxes", which are sealed packages containing random items from various series. With a 600% increase in market value on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (HKSE), POP MART has become a major player in the collectibles industry. Their digital platform POP NOW allows users to virtually open these blind boxes online, where collectors and fans can browse series, select boxes, and receive hints about what's NOT in each box before making their purchase decision. The excitement comes from not knowing which collectible figure you'll get until you open it, making strategic purchasing decisions crucial for users who want specific rare figures.

Problem?

POP NOW users want to be confident in which blind box to pick.

POP NOW is a function from POP MART that allows users to open blind boxes virtually. While POP NOW allows users to know a set number of which specific items are not found in a box, users don't have the time to calculate the likelihood of their choice item appearing in a blind box. Users only find out which item they got after purchase.

Prediction

Clear Visual Probability Results

Users can select their desired figure and instantly see their chances presented through clear, easy-to-read visualizations. The pie chart shows overall probability distribution across all boxes, while the bar chart highlights the top 5 boxes for a specific figure. This transforms complex probability calculations into simple, actionable insights that help users make confident decisions within their 5-minute window.

By presenting probabilities through clear visual charts, users can instantly understand which boxes offer the best chances for their desired figure. The pie chart provides a quick overview of all box probabilities, while the bar chart focuses on the top contenders. This eliminates the guesswork and time-consuming manual calculations, allowing users to make data-driven decisions quickly during their limited 5-minute window.
